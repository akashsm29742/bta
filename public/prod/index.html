Choose The Best Plan For You

BANGALORE TENNIS ACADEMY is designed for you to enjoy the experience and to be motivated to play your best. It offers a systematic world class coaching method in a fun, dynamic atmosphere whether you are an adult, junior, beginner or advanced.

MINI TENNIS PROGRAM

MTA

Group training which includes fitness, co-ordination & improvement drill techniques, Evaluation after one month

ADVANCED BEGINNERS PROGRAM

ABP

Group training includes – fitness drills and co-ordination drills, Speed group training with senior coach twice a week, Evaluation – 15 days

TOURNAMENT PLAYER’S PROGRAM

TTP

Group training – 2 hours in evening ,Group training – 1 hour in morning, Technical corrections with senior coach twice a week – one to one, Evaluation of performance after 15 days

INTERMEDIATE PLAYERS PROGRAM

IPP

Group training daily for 1 hour, Group fitness daily – 20 minutes, Evaluation after 15 days, Matches after 15 days on weekdays/ Sunday

WEEKEND PROGRAM FOR ADULTS/MEMBERS

WPA

Group training daily for 1 hour, Group fitness daily – 20 minutes

WEEKEND PROGRAM FOR LADIES

WPL

Group training daily for 1 hour,Group fitness daily– 20 minutes

INTENSIVE TRAINING PROGRAM

ITP

Group training for 2 hours in morning and 1 hour in the evening, Group fitness training for 1 hour daily, Technical correction with head coach twice a week individually, Evaluation of performance every 15 days

WEEKEND PROGRAM FOR KIDS

WPK

Group training daily for 1 hour, Group fitness daily - 20 minutes, Evaluation of performance every 15 days
